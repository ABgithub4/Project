Electric vehicles for Environmental sustainability

How beneficiary are E-cars as opposed to their counterparts?

This website explains the vast benefits associated with E-cars as the transport industry sways to a more sustainable future.

Why you should go electric:

  • Lower servicing and maintenance costs
  • Cheaper to refuel than petrol/diesel cars
  • Free parking for electric vehicles in some towns and cities

Electric car types

  • Battery-electric Vehicle (BEV)
  • Plug-in hybrid electric vehicle (PHEV)
  • Extended range electric vehicle (E-REV)

Sustainability

  • Increased Air Quality
  • Less maintenance costs
  • Lower emissions - attractive for London ULEZ zones.
